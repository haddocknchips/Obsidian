### GARDP did not provide grant funding

- Jeff's 15 Apr 2026 email to DFC:
	- Aurigene - the manufacturer of our FDA-approved treatment Zoli, this firm is a fully-fledged Contract Development and Manufacturing Organization (CDMO), meaning its business model already assumes ownership and financing of manufacturing infrastructure across multiple clients. Unlike fragile generic suppliers facing demand uncertainty, a CDMO does not represent a market‑failure case that would justify CAPEX grants from GARDP. Providing such funding would risk subsidising a commercial platform, distorting incentives, and cross‑subsidising non‑GARDP products. (This is why **GARDP’s support in the Zoli case has focused on CMC optimisation (chemistry, manufacturing and controls)** and access rather than manufacturing CAPEX.)
