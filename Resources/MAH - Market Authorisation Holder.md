## Definition

- The **legal entity named on the marketing authorisation** granted by a regulator (EMA, FDA, national authority)
- The **single point of regulatory accountability** for the product in that jurisdiction 

## Key responsibilities

- Holds and maintains the marketing authorisation
- Full responsibility for:
    - Pharmacovigilance
    - Regulatory compliance and variations
    - Safety communications and recalls
    - Product labelling and approved indications
- Liaison with regulators throughout the product lifecycle   

## What the MAH does _not_ need to do

- Does not need to manufacture
- Does not need to distribute
- Does not need to own IP

## Risk profile

- Highest legal and regulatory liability
- Ongoing fixed costs even at low volumes
