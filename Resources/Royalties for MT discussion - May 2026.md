### Three royalty modalities

- In-licensing agreement
	- Add asset to GARDP portfolio
	- GARDP funds clinical, non-clinical (examples?) and CMC activites in exchange for manufacturing and commercialisation rights
- Commercial out-licensing agreement
	- Commercial partners distribute and commercialise GARDP's in-license products in GARDP territories
	- <u>Royalties are applied to net sales or net profits</u> (validated by GARDP SPC)
	- No financial mechanism for manufacturers; they have to invest in CAPEX or they act as CDMOs 
- Development out-licensing agreement
	- Products where GARDP has benerated new IP (eg, apramycin and products from DER programme), out-licensing model follows principles closer to classical pharmaceutical structure adapted for AB market
	- Partner will manage clinical activities
