### Lessons learned 

- Collaborate with key players eg, CARB-X to streamline efforts and use funds efficiently
- Tailor support for antibiotic R&D and access partners
	- Working with different partners and public and private sector
- Seek manufacturing rights and prioritise chemistry, manufacturing and controls; CMC has been seen as an Achilles heel of many projects in the sector
- Take active leadership role, with clear decision rights, in pharmaceutical and clinical development; we don't see ourselves as a pass-through
- Will launch a new 2027 strategy
