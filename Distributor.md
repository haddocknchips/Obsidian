## What it is

- An entity authorised to **sell, market, import, and/or distribute** the product in a territory

## Key responsibilities

- Physical supply chain
- Sales to hospitals, wholesalers, governments
- May handle promotion (within label)
- Must comply with GDP and local commercial law

## What it does _not_ do

- Does not hold regulatory approval
- Does not manage pharmacovigilance (except reporting obligations)
- Does not control indications or label

## Risk profile

- Operational and credit risk
- Low regulatory liability
