## 1) What “SPV / fund‑based pull hybrids” mean in antibiotic terms (and why they matter)

A **pull hybrid** (in the way your SECURE work uses the concept) is **not just one payment** (e.g., a subscription). It is a **financing vehicle**—often implementable as a “stand‑alone” structure or hosted in an existing platform—that **combines**:

1. a **delinkage / revenue guarantee logic** for suppliers (the “pull” element), _and_
2. **financial intermediation** that solves the “who pays first” problem for country procurement and access (the “fund / SPV” element). ## 2) The most concrete SPV‑style hybrid already on the table: SECURE’s results‑based pre‑financing facility + delinkage

### 2.1 The “fund/SPV” spine: a results‑based pre‑financing facility

In [20200331_GARDP_Summary deck](https://paolosison-my.sharepoint.com/personal/paolo_sison_io/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7B7AE655DD-5C00-42B7-A021-B3A77411614C%7D&file=20200331_GARDP_Summary%20deck.pptx&action=edit&mobileredirect=true), SECURE’s investor proposition is described as a **results‑based pre‑financing facility** where **investors provide loans to countries**, and **countries reimburse only if and when expected results are achieved** (i.e., output‑based indicators tied to SECURE functioning such as timely delivery, quality, and registration share). [[20200331_G...mmary deck | PowerPoint]](https://paolosison-my.sharepoint.com/personal/paolo_sison_io/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7B7AE655DD-5C00-42B7-A021-B3A77411614C%7D&file=20200331_GARDP_Summary%20deck.pptx&action=edit&mobileredirect=true&DefaultItemOpen=1)

Key risk‑sharing features are also specified:

- Risks are mitigated by **pooling countries** and a **50% first‑loss guarantee** provided by development funders. [[20200331_G...mmary deck | PowerPoint]](https://paolosison-my.sharepoint.com/personal/paolo_sison_io/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7B7AE655DD-5C00-42B7-A021-B3A77411614C%7D&file=20200331_GARDP_Summary%20deck.pptx&action=edit&mobileredirect=true&DefaultItemOpen=1), [[KOIS SECUR...report (1) | PDF]](https://paolosison-my.sharepoint.com/personal/paolo_sison_io/Documents/Microsoft%20Copilot%20Chat%20Files/Copilot%20Notebook%20Uploads/KOIS%20SECURE%20financing_Final%20report%20\(1\).pdf?web=1)
- Development funders provide **grant subsidies** including **grants to cover the interests** (investor premium) and, potentially, **co‑payments for antibiotic provision** in low‑income settings. [[20200331_G...mmary deck | PowerPoint]](https://paolosison-my.sharepoint.com/personal/paolo_sison_io/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7B7AE655DD-5C00-42B7-A021-B3A77411614C%7D&file=20200331_GARDP_Summary%20deck.pptx&action=edit&mobileredirect=true&DefaultItemOpen=1), [[KOIS SECUR...report (1) | PDF]](https://paolosison-my.sharepoint.com/personal/paolo_sison_io/Documents/Microsoft%20Copilot%20Chat%20Files/Copilot%20Notebook%20Uploads/KOIS%20SECURE%20financing_Final%20report%20\(1\).pdf?web=1)
- The model anticipates that **after the first three years**, countries transition to **direct payments** (e.g., annual subscription) for antibiotic provision and for some SECURE interventions (forecasting/procurement support and capacity building). [[20200331_G...mmary deck | PowerPoint]](https://paolosison-my.sharepoint.com/personal/paolo_sison_io/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7B7AE655DD-5C00-42B7-A021-B3A77411614C%7D&file=20200331_GARDP_Summary%20deck.pptx&action=edit&mobileredirect=true&DefaultItemOpen=1), [[KOIS SECUR...report (1) | PDF]](https://paolosison-my.sharepoint.com/personal/paolo_sison_io/Documents/Microsoft%20Copilot%20Chat%20Files/Copilot%20Notebook%20Uploads/KOIS%20SECURE%20financing_Final%20report%20\(1\).pdf?web=1)

This is “SPV‑like” because it behaves like a **dedicated financing facility** that aggregates capital, sets eligibility/repayment triggers, and channels funds to procurement while separating (and allocating) risk.

### 2.2 The “pull” layer: delinking revenue from volumes + minimum revenue logic

In [KOIS SECURE financing_Final report (1)](https://paolosison-my.sharepoint.com/personal/paolo_sison_io/Documents/Microsoft%20Copilot%20Chat%20Files/Copilot%20Notebook%20Uploads/KOIS%20SECURE%20financing_Final%20report%20\(1\).pdf), the economic model is framed as **delinking revenue and volumes to encourage stewardship**. It lists several **de‑linkage structures** (Market Entry Reward, Transferable Exclusivity Extensions, “Pay or play”, Minimum Guaranteed Revenue) and states key objectives such as **guaranteeing minimum revenues** so antibiotic developers/manufacturers/distributors can survive the commercialization “valley of death,” safeguarding access despite unprofitable markets, and reducing pressure to sell volume. [[KOIS SECUR...report (1) | PDF]](https://paolosison-my.sharepoint.com/personal/paolo_sison_io/Documents/Microsoft%20Copilot%20Chat%20Files/Copilot%20Notebook%20Uploads/KOIS%20SECURE%20financing_Final%20report%20\(1\).pdf?web=1)

The report also highlights a specific point that’s crucial for a fund/SPV hybrid: it flags **Minimum Guaranteed Revenue** as a **fixed compensation de‑linked from sales, guaranteed over time**, and labels it as a “Relevant model for GARDP.” [[KOIS SECUR...report (1) | PDF]](https://paolosison-my.sharepoint.com/personal/paolo_sison_io/Documents/Microsoft%20Copilot%20Chat%20Files/Copilot%20Notebook%20Uploads/KOIS%20SECURE%20financing_Final%20report%20\(1\).pdf?web=1)

**Put together**, you get a clear hybrid:

- A **financing facility** (investor + donor‑backed) that gets countries into procurement early and shifts payment later (and conditionally), _while_
- A **delinked compensation logic** that can give manufacturers predictable revenue without incentivizing overuse. [[KOIS SECUR...report (1) | PDF]](https://paolosison-my.sharepoint.com/personal/paolo_sison_io/Documents/Microsoft%20Copilot%20Chat%20Files/Copilot%20Notebook%20Uploads/KOIS%20SECURE%20financing_Final%20report%20\(1\).pdf?web=1), [[20200331_G...mmary deck | PowerPoint]](https://paolosison-my.sharepoint.com/personal/paolo_sison_io/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7B7AE655DD-5C00-42B7-A021-B3A77411614C%7D&file=20200331_GARDP_Summary%20deck.pptx&action=edit&mobileredirect=true&DefaultItemOpen=1)

## 3) The operational “plumbing” that makes the hybrid credible: pooled procurement + a coordinating entity

A fund/SPV hybrid is only as strong as its ability to **execute procurement and supply** reliably (otherwise the outcome triggers fail).

The [KOIS SECURE financing_Final report (1)](https://paolosison-my.sharepoint.com/personal/paolo_sison_io/Documents/Microsoft%20Copilot%20Chat%20Files/Copilot%20Notebook%20Uploads/KOIS%20SECURE%20financing_Final%20report%20\(1\).pdf) outlines integrated supply chain models and suggests that more integrated approaches (from **group contracting** to **fully integrated models**) are preferred in antibiotics because they increase visibility to developers and manufacturers through **pooled contracting and joint procurement**. [[KOIS SECUR...report (1) | PDF]](https://paolosison-my.sharepoint.com/personal/paolo_sison_io/Documents/Microsoft%20Copilot%20Chat%20Files/Copilot%20Notebook%20Uploads/KOIS%20SECURE%20financing_Final%20report%20\(1\).pdf?web=1), [[KOIS SECUR...report (1) | PDF]](https://paolosison-my.sharepoint.com/personal/paolo_sison_io/Documents/Microsoft%20Copilot%20Chat%20Files/Copilot%20Notebook%20Uploads/KOIS%20SECURE%20financing_Final%20report%20\(1\).pdf?web=1)

It also defines roles such as a **Procurement coordinator** that “purchases drugs on behalf of members” and establishes supply arrangements, contracting responsibilities, and supports capacity building at country level (logistics, distribution, monitoring). [[KOIS SECUR...report (1) | PDF]](https://paolosison-my.sharepoint.com/personal/paolo_sison_io/Documents/Microsoft%20Copilot%20Chat%20Files/Copilot%20Notebook%20Uploads/KOIS%20SECURE%20financing_Final%20report%20\(1\).pdf?web=1)

And it provides examples of **central purchasing / contracting initiatives** (Gavi, Global Fund, PAHO revolving mechanisms, etc.), reinforcing that this kind of pooled procurement platform is a real precedent—even if antibiotics have distinct stewardship constraints. [[KOIS SECUR...report (1) | PDF]](https://paolosison-my.sharepoint.com/personal/paolo_sison_io/Documents/Microsoft%20Copilot%20Chat%20Files/Copilot%20Notebook%20Uploads/KOIS%20SECURE%20financing_Final%20report%20\(1\).pdf?web=1)

**Why this matters for SPV design:** a financing SPV/fund can be lean if it can**“plug into”** an existing procurement platform; otherwise it must replicate procurement capacity (which adds governance/ops burden).

## 5) Three fund/SPV hybrid “archetypes” you can lift directly from the SECURE materials

### Archetype A — **Outcome‑linked Procurement Financing Facility (the SECURE model)**

- Investors lend to countries through a facility; repayment triggered by output/process indicators; donor first‑loss + interest subsidies.  
    **What it buys you:** early adoption, reduced upfront risk for countries, and a clear role for catalytic donor capital. [[20200331_G...mmary deck | PowerPoint]](https://paolosison-my.sharepoint.com/personal/paolo_sison_io/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7B7AE655DD-5C00-42B7-A021-B3A77411614C%7D&file=20200331_GARDP_Summary%20deck.pptx&action=edit&mobileredirect=true&DefaultItemOpen=1), [[KOIS SECUR...report (1) | PDF]](https://paolosison-my.sharepoint.com/personal/paolo_sison_io/Documents/Microsoft%20Copilot%20Chat%20Files/Copilot%20Notebook%20Uploads/KOIS%20SECURE%20financing_Final%20report%20\(1\).pdf?web=1)

### Archetype B — **Pooled Subscription / Minimum Revenue Guarantee Fund**

- A pooled model across countries with **partial delinkage** and an aggregate volume threshold; provides predictable revenue to suppliers without tying returns to sales.  
    **What it buys you:** a “pull‑like” stability mechanism focused on LMIC access and stewardship. [[KOIS SECUR...report (1) | PDF]](https://paolosison-my.sharepoint.com/personal/paolo_sison_io/Documents/Microsoft%20Copilot%20Chat%20Files/Copilot%20Notebook%20Uploads/KOIS%20SECURE%20financing_Final%20report%20\(1\).pdf?web=1)

### Archetype C — **Procurement Platform + Financing Wrapper (hosted model)**

- KOIS explicitly contemplates selecting the “best fitting hosting option (stand‑alone, existing financing platforms, multilaterals, etc.)” when structuring the financing solution.  
    **What it buys you:** speed and lower operational burden if you can host within an existing platform while the “fund” piece handles risk‑sharing and capital. [[20200331_G...mmary deck | PowerPoint]](https://paolosison-my.sharepoint.com/personal/paolo_sison_io/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7B7AE655DD-5C00-42B7-A021-B3A77411614C%7D&file=20200331_GARDP_Summary%20deck.pptx&action=edit&mobileredirect=true&DefaultItemOpen=1)
