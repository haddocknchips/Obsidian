## What it is

- The entity that **holds contractual rights** (via licence or assignment) to:
    - Use the IP
    - Manufacture
    - Seek regulatory approval
    - Commercialise in defined territories

## Key responsibilities

- Defined by **private contract**, not regulators
- May include:
    - Funding development
    - Technology transfer
    - Market development
    - Appointing MAHs and distributors

## Important nuance

- A license holder **may or may not** be the MAH
- In many access models:
    - The license holder **appoints** or **becomes** the MAH
    - Or sublicenses to a local MAH

## Risk profile

- Commercial and contractual risk
- No direct regulatory liability unless also MAH
